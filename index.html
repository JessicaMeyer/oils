What Are Essential Oils?

Essential Oils are made from plants! Think: the rind, shrub, root, herb, flower, seed or tree. Extracted oils go through a careful steam distillation process, where the molecules become highly concentrated. Pure essential oils has been used for thousands of years to balance the body and support overall wellness.
